MISSION STATEMENT

 

 

To assist The University of the West Indies in fulfilling its mission of unlocking the potential of the West Indian people.

This we will achieve by living and working together as a community of men in the active pursuit of academic excellence, leadership skills, vibrant extra-curricular participation and active service to humanity.

The Lions of Chancellor Hall recognise that as the future leaders of the Caribbean, it is essential that we make our exit from the University as brave men committed to the cause of Caribbean development. This commitment will be instilled in every Chancellorite through unity, love, brotherhood and integrity.

LET THE LIONS ROAR!!!
