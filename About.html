GREETINGS

 

 

Welcome all to this our Chancellor Hall Web page. Chancellor Hall, as many can truthfully attest, has since its inception, served as the premier all of residence on the UWI Mona Campus.

Chancellorites share a rich and colourful history which is punctuated by numerous traditions which we hold dearly and protect fiercely. A few traditions, as one would expect have been modified to keep abreast with the changes in the surrounding environment but for the most part remained intact. As you browse through this site you will be made privy to some of these traditions. You will be exposed to a little of our history and be able to partake of a little of our everyday life. Then you will come to appreciate the Chancellor experience.

We the present Lions, offspring of the Information Age consider it fittingly appropriate to create this website to give you the visitor a peek into Chancellor Hall or you the past Chancellorite a chance to keep in touch. We therefore welcome you and trust that good use will be made of this ground breaking venture. GUIDANCE.

Written by Sean Curtis.
