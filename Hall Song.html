HALL SONG

 

 

Chancellor stands brave and Tall,
Conquering Lions all,
irvine and taylor place,

Clearly above the rest,
To stop us there are none,
Chancellorites will forward on,
Still determined,
As we were yesterday, yesterday, yesterday.
